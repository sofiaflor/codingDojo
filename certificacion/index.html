Welcome to We Collect Your IT

We offer a free collection servise across the UK for the removal of your old IT systems. We safely reuse This equipment by ensuring any sensitive or personal information that was on these machines is either physically remaved or destroyed before we reuse them. Using us shall not only benefit the environment by avoiding the waste and disposal of this equiment but shall also help other, as we donate and proportion of all the equipment we collect.

The environmental and social benefits of reuse do not only help conserve natural resouces. but help reduce air and water pollution caused by hazardous disposal.
